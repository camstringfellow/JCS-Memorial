About the Scholarship Fund

The John Corey Stringfellow Memorial Scholarship Fund is a non-profit organization that raises money for a higher education scholarship.

Scholarships will be awarded annually to the soccer players that best reflect the traits that Corey embellished as an athlete and a team player. In addition, a scholarship will be awarded to a deserving art student for the love of creativity, sculpture and drawing design.

About Corey

Corey's love of life and passion for excelling in everything he did was never more evident than when playing soccer. No matter the outcome of the game you would find Corey with the biggest smile on his face because he knew he was given that day to live to the fullest.
